Service Hub

I developed Service Hub using PHP, designed to bridge the gap between service providers such as chefs, security guards, and maids, and those in need of their services. The platform allows service providers, many of whom may be illiterate, to easily create profiles and make themselves available for booking. On the other side, everyday users can browse, select, and hire these service providers with ease. This project emphasizes my ability to create accessible, user-friendly solutions while addressing real-world social challenges through technology.
